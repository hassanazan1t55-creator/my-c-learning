### Day 27: Bitwise Shifts (Left Shift & Right Shift)
* **Folder:** `Day_27_Bitwise_Shifts`
* **File:** `bitwise_shifts.c`
* **What I Learnt:**
  * Use Left Shift (`<<`) to slide bits left, effectively multiplying the integer by 2 per step.
    > Bits ko left shift kar ke number ko double ($ \times 2$) karne ka shortcut seekha.
  * Use Right Shift (`>>`) to slide bits right, effectively dividing the integer by 2 per step.
    > Bits ko right shift kar ke number ko aadha ($ / 2$) karne ka logic samjha.
  * Predict exact 8-bit output states like `00010100` for advanced hardware level math.
    > Custom bit movement ko binary level par successfully trace karna seekha.
